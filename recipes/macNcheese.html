Mac And Cheese

Your favourite comfort food in 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked elbow pasta
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbs butter
  • 2.5 cups shredded cheese
  • 0.5 cup whole milk

Steps

  1. Put pasta,water and salt into pot and cook for 4 minutes
  2. Drain and gently stir in butter until melted. Add cheese and milk(2-3 tbs at a time)
  3. Add seasoning to taste and let it sit for 10 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
