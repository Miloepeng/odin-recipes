ABC Soup

A hearty and nutritious soup for rainy days

The recipe is suitable for beginners and can be prepared within 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 large potato
  • 1 large white onion
  • 3 chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 tsp chicken boullion powder
  • 1/4 tsp sesame oil
  • salt(to taste)
  • water(sufficient)

Steps

  1. Peel vegetables and cut into chunks
  2. Parboil your choice of protein
  3. In a separate pot,add in your protein and vegetables and pour enough water to cover ingredients
  4. Once your pot comes to a boil, let it simmer for 30-45minutes
  5. Season with salt, white pepper, chicken boullion powder to taste, and enjoy!
